Taj Mahal

General Info

The Taj Mahal is one of the old seven wonders of the world! It is located in India, Agra. The Taj Mahal used to have many jewels built into the walls. It also had a huge courtyard which tourists may be walking on at this very moment!

History

At the time India had been at peace but were then attacked by the British. They then took control and ransacked many places, one of which was the Taj Mahal. They chiseled many valuables in the walls such as lapis lazuli, emeralds, rubies, and even tiny diamonds. Air pollution has recently been a major leading facter in the decay of the Taj Mahal. Starting from the 20th century, there was a major restoration to bring back the former glory of the Taj Mahal!
